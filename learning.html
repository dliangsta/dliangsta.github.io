guest@DAVIDWLIANG:/learning

>> David.learning I'm always excited to learn more, whether its about computer science, cubing, or just anything in general, so I thought I'd make a page to share what I'm learning!

>> David.learning_cs I'm currently learning Angular 2 and Node.js and hoping to work that into this website once I finish my Udemy course. Next after that is improving my C++ skills with a Unity Udemy course! Meanwhile, I'll be learning more about databases and working one into my Tetris game to keep high scores for visitors to my website in addition to my AI!

>> David.learning_cubing I'm learning VLS (valk last slot) which is basically a way to skip OLL (orientation of last layer; 2nd last step) from any edge orientation of the top layer. There are a lot of algorithms and a lot of cases so this will take quite a while to learn but will certainly be well worth it! I'm also working on my F2L efficiency, since that's really important. Additionally, I spend a little bit of time each day practicing color neutral solves (start with any color on the bottom) and extended cross (finishing one F2L pair at the same time as the cross)!

>> David.learning_other Outside of CS and cubing, I'm learning a few other skills. I'm hoping to become a better cook not only for myself but for my friends too! My current favorite dishes to cook are chicken curry with rice and egg sandwiches. I'm also dabbling with lock picking (for fun, no criminal intent, I promise!). I'm also going to be learning how to invest money because that's a really important life skill to have in the future!
